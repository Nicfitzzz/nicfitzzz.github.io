“The Process”

A quick and dirty on what I do when I build projects.

Problem Solving

Using my trusty Moleskine®, I write down the problem, and organize my thoughts into wireframes and words.

Responsively Coding

Once I'm ready to start building, I start mobile first, top to bottom. From there I progressively enhance, and “keep it clean”.

Tweaking & Troubleshooting

Lastly, I do cross browser and device checks, making sure things are perfectly pixelated in place.
